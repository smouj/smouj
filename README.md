## ⚡ About Me

Building a **mythical ecosystem** of modular AI skills — **local-first, powerful, and 100% open-source**.  
Every skill is a building block. Together, they form **autonomous SuperAgents** capable of real-world automation without cloud lock-in, mandatory paid APIs, or privacy compromises.

| 🧠 AI Agent Architect | 🔒 Privacy-First Builder | ⚙️ Open-Source Advocate | 🚀 SuperAgent Designer |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| Designing modular skill ecosystems | Zero data leaks by design | No lock-in, ever | Coordinated multi-agent systems |
